About

Hi, my name is Dinah!

I’m currently enrolled in the UX certificate program at UCLA Extension. Prior to that, I earned a degree in Graphic Design at Florida Atlantic University. In the middle of all that, I spent a lot of time working in retail as a merchandiser, where I learned a lot about empathy and finding ways to meet the customer’s needs and while still meeting the needs of the business.

In my free time I like to take photos, take care of my plants, read about Meyers Briggs (I’m an INFJ) and play games on my Nintendo Switch.
